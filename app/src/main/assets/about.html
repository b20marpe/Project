Project

About:

This app is an list of my personal favorite dogs with links to the wikipedia page and a picture of a the specific breed that you press in the list.

Target audience:

Beacuse my project is information about dogs, the targeted audience is everyone who likes dogs.

There are both males and females of all ages that like dogs which means they might want to get information about dogs, which they can get in my project app.
